Our Mission

Why EcoSwap Exists

Every year, millions of usable items are thrown away because people don't have a simple way to connect with others who need them. EcoSwap was built to solve this — a digital marketplace focused on sustainability, community, and innovation.

We believe technology should serve people and the planet. By making it easy to swap and sell second-hand goods locally, we reduce the demand for new production, lower carbon emissions, and build stronger local economies.

Our platform aligns directly with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 9 (SDG9) — promoting inclusive and sustainable industrialization, fostering innovation, and building resilient infrastructure.

UN Sustainable Development Goal

SDG9 — Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure

Resilient Infrastructure

EcoSwap builds inclusive digital infrastructure that gives all Malaysians access to a sustainable marketplace, regardless of location or income level.

Sustainable Industrialization

By extending the lifecycle of consumer goods through swapping and reselling, EcoSwap reduces the industrial demand for new resource extraction and manufacturing.

Platform Overview

How EcoSwap Works

A simple, secure process from listing to swap completion.

1

Create Your Account

Register securely using email or Google. Your profile and data are stored safely in Firebase with full authentication.

2

List Your Item

Post your unused items with photos, description, condition, and location. Choose to swap or sell.

3

Browse & Discover

Search listings by category, location, or use the interactive map to find items near you.

4

Send a Swap Request

Found something you like? Send a swap request with a message. The seller reviews and responds.

5

Complete the Transaction

Accept a swap or proceed to secure checkout powered by Stripe. Meet up locally or arrange delivery.
